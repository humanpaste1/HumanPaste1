Integrated Campaign Advertising

Vogogo Marketing Q3 2015 - Q2 2016 | One Admin. Hundreds of Tools.

Background:

As Vogogo continued to expand its risk management platform a new creative campaign was established to showcase the leading-edge features and comprehensive functionality now offered. This creative was illustrated through outbound marketing advertising and business collateral in conjunction with an inbound content strategy and online advertising.

Print Advertising

In the fall of 2015 to support Vogogo's booth presence at Money 2020, established the 2-page truck ad that was positioned on page 3-4 of the yBitcoin Magazine that provided seat drops in the presentation halls each day during the conference.

WHAT WAS RECEIVED:
• 2,000+ seat drop per day at Money 2020 2015
• Increase in Vogogo website traffic and time on website
• Increased awareness for business development and sales

Business Collateral

Developed to support Vogogo's direct business sales and product integration teams this brochure showcased the multi-layered features and functionality of the Vogogo fraud mitigation and payment processing services. Specically highlighting product differenitators in the Know Your Customer (KYC), global compliance, merchant underwriting and customizable API integrations.
